PhyAi Concept

Generate Math & Physics equations by observing
the real world and research papers

Motivation (Why)

PhyAi would be an attempt to build a AI Physist. Just like a human Physist this AI will try to model the real world observations in form of beautiful Mathematical equations.

Techtics (What & How)

We will utilize Computer Vison to observe the real world objects and attempt to convert the observations to equations. Additionally, The PhyAI will scan through journals and research papers and try to mathematically corelate any dependencies that might have been missed by human researchers because that is a different stream or subject. Just like Albert Einstien hinted the presence of molecules based on brownian motion.

Reflection & Application (now & next)

An AI that will observe the physical aspects of nature and translate them into beautiful mathematical equations. Who knows it might conclude on something obscure that may endup stitching Quantum world and Space-time
