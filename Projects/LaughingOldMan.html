Laughing Old Man

An experiment with
Deepfake First-Order GAN &
Digital Ageing

Motivation (Why)

As part of our course on Experiments with AI Generated Media we were posed with a pro level challenge - to use the first-order models for video generation create a Create a deepfake of your younger/older self talking back to your present self!

Techtics (What & How)

Creating the young self seem pretty simple and easy, would need to use an photograph of my younger version. But I wanted to have some fun and make it a bit more challenging, so I decided to digitally age myself and then make that version talk from the future!

For Aging myself, used the Lifespan Age Transformation Synthesis trained GAN model to de-age and age myself from my current photograph.
Then used the last frame of the DeepFake Old myself and applied it to the First Order GAN model to manipulate a static digitally aged photograph of mine using a recorded video with facial movements.
Finally, stiched a video with Message from the Future from old me to the young me.

Reflection & Application (now & next)

The result was awesome, while a bit freaky as to think that this level of manipulation can be done in current times and this is going to become more flawless in the future.
But for now, this was done just to learn and have fun
